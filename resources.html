Useful things to learn:

  • Become familiar with a variety of motors and how they work (e.g. stepper, linear actuators, brushed dc, brushless dc, servos, etc)
  • Become familiar with gear trains and how they work
  • Know how tank drive systems work
  • Learn how to work in the machine shop or assist with assembly (contact us to get more info about shop training sessions)
